UKRP Staff Handbook

Welcome to the UK Roleplay (UKRP) Staff Handbook. This handbook defines authority, punishments, structure, and expectations.

Punishments

  • Minor offenses → Warning / Jail
  • Moderate offenses → Kick / Temp Ban
  • Severe offenses → Permanent Ban
  • Exploiting → Immediate Permanent Ban

Chain of Command

  • Ownership
  • Board / Directors
  • Executive Staff
  • Management
  • Administration
  • Moderation
  • Trainees

Chain bypassing is forbidden unless an emergency exists.

Ownership

Ownership retains absolute authority over UKRP. All final decisions rest with ownership.

Staff Conduct

  • Professional behavior at all times
  • No abuse of power
  • No leaks or favoritism
  • All actions must be justified
